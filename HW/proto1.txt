Proto1 - BOM
-------
Arduino Mega
breadboard, jumpers
MOSFET transistor
Diode
Push button
10k Resistor
12V Power supply
Pull Solenoid with enough force at 5mm stroke. (*)
Legos

(*) I used SMO-0837 from http://www.tai-shing.com.tw/EN/solenoid/solenoid-Solenoid-open.cfm
which has just barely enough force (800g at 1mm stroke, 180g at 5mm stroke, power 4W). More would be better
